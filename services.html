Everything your boat needs

From spring launch to winter storage and every repair in between — all from one yard, handled by a crew you’ll get to know season after season.

Service 01

Haul-Out & Launch

When the season turns, our crew handles the heavy lifting — hauling your boat out in the fall and launching it again in spring, on a schedule that keeps you on the water when it matters and protected when it doesn’t.

  • Fall haul-out and spring launch
  • Scheduling that beats the seasonal rush
  • Careful handling, from small craft to cruisers

Service 02

Service & Repair

From routine maintenance to new engine installs, marine wiring, trailer repairs and on-water testing, our technicians keep your boat running right. We work across the major engine and electronics brands and are known for going the extra mile to get the job done.

  • Engine installs, tune-ups and diagnostics
  • Marine wiring and electronics
  • Trailer repairs and general boatyard work
Service 03

Winter Storage & Winterizing

A Nova Scotia winter is hard on a boat that isn’t put away properly. We winterize engines and systems and store your boat through the off-season so it’s ready to go — not nursing damage — when the weather warms up again.

  • Engine and systems winterizing
  • Off-season storage
  • Spring recommissioning and launch

Service 04

Detailing

We bring boats back to life — washing, waxing, buffing and polishing gel coat, touch-up paint and minor fiberglass work. Whether you’re getting ready to sell or just want her looking her best, we’ll make her shine.

  • Exterior and interior cleaning
  • Buffing, polishing and gel-coat restoration
  • Touch-up paint and minor fiberglass
Service 05

Boat Sales, Brokerage & Financing

We carry a rotating selection of quality used boats, inspected and ready for the water, and we can help you sell yours. Found the one you want? We offer financing on boat purchases, subject to approval, so you can get on the water sooner.

  • Inspected used boats for sale
  • Help selling your current boat
  • Financing available (subject to approval)

When the weather turns

Storm Haul-Out

When a hurricane or major storm is forecast, getting your boat out of the water quickly can save it. Our crew mobilizes ahead of severe weather to haul boats to safety — the same way we’ve worked into the evening before past storms to get every boat we can onto safe ground.

If a storm is coming, call us as early as you can.
