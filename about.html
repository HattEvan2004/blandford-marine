Take care of the boat, and the people who love it

On the shore at Hubbards, Nova Scotia, Blandford Marine is a full-service marine shop and boat dealership built around one idea.

Our story

One yard for the whole life of a boat

We handle the whole life of a boat — helping you find and finance the right one, keeping it running with expert service, hauling and launching it each season, storing it safely through winter, and bringing back its shine with detailing.

When a storm bears down on the coast, we’re the crew working into the evening to get boats out of harm’s way. What sets us apart is the people: an experienced local crew, and customers who come back year after year — some from across the border.

We’re proud to be part of this community, from the boats we launch each spring to the working fleets we cheer on every Dumping Day.
