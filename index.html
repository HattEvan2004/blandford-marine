What we do

Everything your boat needs, in one place

From spring launch to winter storage and every repair in between — handled by a crew you’ll get to know, season after season.

Haul-Out & Launch

We handle the heavy lifting each season — out in the fall, back in the spring, on a schedule that keeps you on the water when it matters.

Service & Repair

Routine maintenance, engine installs, marine wiring, trailer repairs and on-water testing across the major brands.

Winter Storage

We winterize engines and systems and store your boat so it’s ready to go — not nursing damage — come spring.

Detailing

Washing, waxing, buffing and polishing gel coat, touch-up paint and minor fiberglass — we’ll make her shine.

Delivery & Transport

Regional boat delivery and seasonal transport for owners coming and going each year, including our returning out-of-province customers.

When the weather turns

Storm Haul-Out

When a storm is forecast, getting your boat out fast can save it. Our crew mobilizes ahead of severe weather. If a storm is coming, call us.
