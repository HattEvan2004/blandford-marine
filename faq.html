Frequently asked questions

The quick answers. For anything specific to your boat, give us a call — we’re happy to help.

Where are you located, and who do you serve?

We’re at 19 Firehall Rd in Hubbards, NS. We serve Hubbards, Blandford, Chester, Lunenburg, and the wider St. Margaret’s Bay and Mahone Bay region.

What services do you offer?

Boat sales and financing, service and repair, haul-out and launch, winterizing and winter storage, detailing, and delivery — all from one yard.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — we offer financing on boat purchases, subject to approval. Ask us about your options.

Can you store my boat for the winter?

Yes. We haul, winterize, and store boats through the off-season, then launch them again in spring. Booking ahead is the best way to lock in your spot at the busy times of year.

Do you sell used boats? Can you help me sell mine?

We carry a rotating selection of quality used boats, and we can help you sell yours. Inventory changes through the season, so call for what’s available right now.

A storm is coming — can you haul my boat out fast?

When severe weather is forecast we prioritize getting boats out of the water. Call us as early as you can — the sooner we know, the better we can help.

What engine and electronics brands do you service?

We work across the major brands, including Mercury / MerCruiser, Yamaha, Honda and Volvo Penta, along with Raymarine and Garmin electronics.

What are your hours?

Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM. Hours can shift a little with the season — call ahead if you’re making a special trip.

Still have a question?

Give us a call or send a message — we’re happy to help.
